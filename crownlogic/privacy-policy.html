Privacy Policy

Crown Logic ("we", "us", or "our") is a free-to-download app that offers optional in-app purchases. This app is intended for use as is.

Crown Logic does not require an account, does not collect personal information directly, and does not operate any servers. All game data (progress, settings, leaderboard times) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted.

However, the app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Links to the privacy policies of these third-party service providers:

Advertising

Crown Logic displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This data collection is governed by Google's privacy policy linked above.

In-App Purchases

Crown Logic offers optional in-app purchases. Purchases are processed entirely through the platform's payment system (Google Play Billing on Android, Apple StoreKit on iOS). We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information. All purchase processing is handled by Google or Apple in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Bluetooth

Crown Logic uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for local multiplayer. Bluetooth is used only to discover and connect with nearby players on the same local network. No data is transmitted to external servers through Bluetooth. You can decline the Bluetooth permission and still use all other features of the app.
